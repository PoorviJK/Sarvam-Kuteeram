About Us

In Sarvam Kuteeram, located in Bangalore, Karnataka, we emphasize an ancient Indian educational tradition, holistic learning, and character development. At Gurukul Learning Space, we strive to preserve and adapt this traditional system to empower young minds in the modern world.

Sarvam Kuteeram provides a nurturing environment where students not only acquire academic knowledge but also learn important life skills, ethics, and values. We believe in the philosophy of "Guru-Shishya Parampara," where the relationship between the teacher (Guru) and the student (Shishya) is deeply respected and cherished, integrating Vedic learning with the future of AI and ML.

With a focus on experiential learning, our curriculum includes subjects such as Vedic studies, yoga, meditation, traditional arts, sciences, math, robotics, life skills, and vocational workshops from experts. Through a blend of theory and practice, we aim to instill a sense of curiosity, critical thinking, and reverence for knowledge in our students.
